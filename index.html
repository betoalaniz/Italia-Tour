Exploring Italy

By clicking on the links, you will be given options through Trip Advisor on things we could potentially do in the following cities. Enjoy!

Milan, Italy

Milan is Italy's leading financial centre and its most prosperous manufacturing and commercial city.

Rome, Italy

Rome is the capital of Italy, it is largest city in the country, and seat of the Roman Catholic Church.

Pisa, Italy

Pisa is one of the most important cities in Tuscany and it is extremely well-known for its famous symbol: "the Leaning Tower".

Florence, Italy

Florence was founded as a Roman military colony about the 1st century bce, well known for its art and learning.

Venice, Italy

Venice is a island city, it was once the centre of a maritime republic. It was the greatest seaport in late medieval Europe.

Frankfurt, Germany

Frankfurt is a pretty modern city. It is home to businesses, skyscrapers and Europe's third largest airport.
